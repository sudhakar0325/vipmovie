Plot:

Raghuvaran, a civil engineering graduate, has been unemployed for four years, determined to work only in his field. He frequently clashes with his father, who views him as irresponsible, while his younger brother, Karthik, thrives in an IT job with their father’s approval. Supported by his mother, Bhuvana, Raghuvaran falls for his neighbor’s daughter, Shalini. Initially, she dislikes him, but she grows to love him after understanding his struggles.
